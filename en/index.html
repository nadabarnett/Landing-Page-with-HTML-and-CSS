Our agency

Transform your communications

Since 2009, Emilie Marsolais Communications inc. public relations agency has showcased local and international businesses through creative and impactful campaigns. Our firm is recognized for its solid expertise in industries such as food, technology, the art of living, decorating, wellbeing, fashion and beauty.



Our mission



At Emilie Marsolais Communications, the emphasis is placed on listening, rigour, transparency and the quality of relationships. We believe the success of a public relations campaign depends first and foremost on our resolute commitment to the brands we represent. Which is why our expertise is at the service of companies and organizations who reflect our ideals and vision of the future. Driven by shared values, we proudly contribute to the success of client initiatives.

15years experience

Émilie Marsolais

The founder

For more than 15 years, Emilie has been creating and developing public relations campaigns for prominent companies and brands. An excellent communicator, she has built a solid network of contacts and carved out an enviable reputation with Quebec media and influencers. Her professionalism, combined with creativity, a structured approach and keen editorial skills have helped raise the profile of a number of companies and brands both locally and abroad. A yoga instructor as well, Émilie enjoys sharing her passion for this exquisite discipline with both young and old.

Our services

Tailored Solutions

Our agency offers a wide range of customized public relations services and solutions, according to the specific needs and objectives of the client.

Strategic Planning and Consulting

Development of communication plans, strategic thinking, brainstorming sessions, consulting services.

Media Campaigns

Brand and Product launches.

Content Creation

Message design, communications tools, press releases.

Event Planning

Organization of media events, press conferences, cocktail parties, store openings, unveilings, media tours.

Traditional Media Relations

Creation of targeted press lists, approach and follow up with traditional media, field requests for information, interview coordination.

Digital Public Relations

Creation of targeted press lists, approach and follow up with digital media, field requests for information, interview coordination.

Partnerships with Influencers (Social Media)

Establishing and coordinating partnerships with local influencers and celebrities, developing concepts (contests, sponsored content, etc.)
